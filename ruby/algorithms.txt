Release 0

- Face the elevator door
- If desired floor is above you
	- press the button on the top to go up
- If desired floor is below you
	- press the button on the bottom to go down
- When elevator doors open
	- Walk inside the elevator
	- Turn 180 degrees 
- Press button for desired floor
- When elevator doors open
	- Exit


Release 1: Floss

- Hold floss cartridge
- Unravel 5 inches of floss
- Wrap floss once around both index fingers
- Open mouth
- Position floss towards back left molar
- Until all crevices clean
	- Use fingers to position floss in between teeth
	- Push floss in between teeth
	- Lift floss out of teeth
	- Move forward one tooth
- Rinse mouth
